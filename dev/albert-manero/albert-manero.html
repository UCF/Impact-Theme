Changing Lives

At UCF, we dream big. Out greatest limitations are those we place on ourselves.

When Albert Manero was young, his parents taught him the importance of making the world a better place. "[They] always encouraged me to use my education to help others and to dream big dreams," he says. "Now I want to inspire others to help engineer hope for the world."

Today he's focused on making a positive impact on the grandest and smallest scales. "Our collaboration with the German Aerospace Center and Argonne National Laboratory has provided incredible research opportunities and innovation," he says. "Learning in such a diverse and globally minded campus prepared me to lead a team spanning nine time zones and across cultural differences."
